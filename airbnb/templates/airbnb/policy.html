Residential Rental Regulation and Administration

Regulatory Authority

The Addis Ababa City Administration oversees rental agreements based on Proclamation No. 1320/2016, Article 29, Sub-article 2.

Definition of Terms
  • "City," "Administration," and "Contractor" are explicitly defined for legal clarity.
Execution
  • Rentals must be conducted through a formal contract, clearly documenting tenant and landlord rights and responsibilities.
Registration and Certification
  • Rental agreements must be registered and validated by designated municipal offices.
  • Required documents include identification, proof of ownership, and the rental contract.
Termination
  • Either party must give a one-month notice before terminating the contract.
Rent Adjustment
  • Rent adjustments may occur annually, subject to an evaluation of market conditions and municipal approval.
Residential Rental Agreement Template
Identification
  • Both parties must provide their full names, addresses, and identification details.
Lease Details
  • Specifies the rental property location, type, duration, monthly rent, and deposit requirements.
Maintenance
  • Tenants are responsible for minor repairs and returning the property in good condition.
Termination
  • Requires a one-month prior notice for contract termination.
Legal Compliance
  • All terms are in accordance with the Ethiopian Civil Code Articles 1889 and 1992.
